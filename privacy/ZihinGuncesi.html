2. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Zihin Güncesi is designed to work completely offline, and all your journal entries and data are stored locally on your device only.

What We Don't Collect:

  • Personal identification information

  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Crash reports or diagnostic information

3. How We Use Information

Since we do not collect any personal information, we do not use, process, or share any personal data. Your journal entries remain completely private and are only accessible to you on your device.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • All your data is stored locally on your device only
  • We do not transmit any data to external servers
  • The app works completely offline after installation
  • Your journal entries are only accessible through the app on your device

Android Auto Backup

Your device may automatically back up app data through Android's built-in backup service (if enabled by you). This is managed entirely by Google/Android and is subject to their privacy policies, not ours. You can control this feature in your device's backup settings.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share any data because we do not collect any data. The app does not have any features for:

  • Sharing journal entries
  • Social media integration
  • Cloud synchronization
  • Email or message sharing

Users may take screenshots of their journal entries using their device's built-in screenshot functionality, but this is entirely under user control.

6. Third-Party Services

Zihin Güncesi does not integrate with or use any third-party services, including:

9. Data Retention and Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

Since we do not collect personal data, traditional data protection rights (access, correction, deletion) are not applicable. However, you have complete control over all data in the app since it's stored locally on your device.
